Hello

With over 15 years design experience in the packaging design industrie I can assist at many stages of your design projects. I am available at an hourly rate for either one-off specific tasks or for entire projects, either working on my own or as a member of your team. I pride myself to have worked for a range of national and international clients, met some fantastic people and was involved in many high profile packaging projects.

My main experience is in Extrusion blowmoulding, blowmoulding, injection moulding and vacuum formed packaging solutions. To me, the 'best solution' is about compromise when necessary and courage when required, ensuring packaging designs that look good, flow through production, behaves well during transport, sits proud on the shelves and give customers the ultimate packaging experience. .

A great presentation with 3 concepts can happen in just a couple of days. Providing there is a good brief to get me started. I am happy to discuss your new packaging project in person or if you are further away by Skype.
